## Severity
- `BLOCKER`: Must be addressed before release; user impact is critical or trust-breaking.
- `HIGH`: Major risk to stability or correctness; schedule immediately after blockers.
- `MEDIUM`: Important for maintainability or medium-term reliability; plan in next iteration.
- `LOW`: Nice-to-have improvements or polish.

## Type
- `reliability`: Issues that threaten uptime, correctness, or user trust.
- `architecture`: Structural concerns that impede maintainability or cross-cutting improvements.
